Our Services

Manicure

Enjoy a soothing soak, nail shaping, cuticle care, and a relaxing hand massage without a polish application. Ideal for maintaining healthy, beautiful nails.

Gel Manicure

Long-lasting and chip-resistant, our gel manicure offers a durable and glossy finish that stays flawless for weeks.

BIAB Nails

Strengthen your natural nails with our BIAB service, which provides a strong, flexible, and natural-looking finish. Ideal for promoting nail growth and durability.

BIAB Nail Extensions

Combine the benefits of BIAB with added length through our BIAB nail extensions. Perfect for those who want a natural yet enhanced look.

Acrylic Nails

Enhance your natural nails with durable and customizable acrylic extensions. Perfect for those looking to add length and strength.

Nail Art

Established in 2022, we provide a wide range of services for both females and males, ensuring everyone can enjoy our luxurious treatments. Our offerings include acrylic nail extensions, pedicures, manicures, gel nails, and more.
